# Press Release — LeadDrive CRM

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

## LeadDrive CRM Announces New Phase of Development and Expansion of Da Vinci AI Capabilities

*Warsaw, Poland — April 2026*

**LeadDrive Inc.**, a European CRM technology company built by Ukrainian engineers, today announced a significant expansion of its AI-powered CRM platform, LeadDrive CRM.

### About LeadDrive CRM

LeadDrive CRM is an intelligent customer relationship management platform that unifies sales, marketing, 7-channel inbox, customer support, finance and analytics on a single platform. At its core is **Da Vinci AI** — a proprietary AI engine that powers 16 intelligent features across all 11 modules, from automated lead scoring and email generation to financial analysis and customer service automation.

### Key Highlights

- **128+ features** across 11 integrated modules
- **Da Vinci AI** with multi-agent orchestration and 3-tier fallback architecture
- **7-channel unified inbox** (Email, WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, SMS, Web Chat)
- **Multi-tenant SaaS** with full data isolation and GDPR compliance
- **3-language support** (English, Russian, Azerbaijani) with more planned

### European Roots, Ukrainian Engineering

LeadDrive CRM was designed and built by a team of experienced Ukrainian software engineers. The company is headquartered in Warsaw, Poland, with its engineering team based in Kyiv, Ukraine.

"We built LeadDrive CRM because we saw an opportunity to create a truly intelligent CRM — one where AI isn't an add-on but the foundation of every workflow," said Rashad Rahimov, CEO of LeadDrive Inc. "Our Ukrainian engineering team brings world-class technical expertise, and our European base ensures we meet the highest standards of data protection and business operations."

### What's New

The latest release includes:
- **Multi-agent AI orchestration** — Da Vinci now routes queries to specialized AI agents for sales, support, and finance
- **WhatsApp Business integration** — full inbound/outbound messaging with auto-reply and escalation
- **Financial module expansion** — invoicing, accounts payable, payment orders, aging analysis
- **Customer portal** — self-service tickets, knowledge base, and Da Vinci chat for end customers
- **VoIP integration** — Twilio-powered calling directly from the CRM

### Availability

LeadDrive CRM is available as a cloud-hosted SaaS platform with a 14-day free trial. Enterprise plans include dedicated support and custom onboarding.

### About LeadDrive Inc.

LeadDrive Inc. is a European technology company headquartered in Warsaw, Poland, with engineering operations in Kyiv, Ukraine. The company develops LeadDrive CRM, an AI-powered customer relationship management platform for IT outsourcing companies and service businesses.
